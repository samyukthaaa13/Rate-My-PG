Resources and Guides

Housing Safety

Living in a safe environment is essential. Here are some tips to ensure safety in your PG accommodations:

  • Always check the surrounding area for security measures such as CCTV cameras.
  • Ask the landlord or PG management about emergency contact numbers.
  • Keep your personal belongings secure and avoid sharing sensitive information with strangers.

Legal Rights

Knowing your legal rights as a tenant is crucial. Here are some key legal rights you should be aware of:

  • Right to privacy and security in your accommodation.
  • Right to a habitable living environment with essential amenities.
  • Right to be free from discrimination based on gender, race, or nationality.

Rental Agreements

Understanding rental agreements will help you make informed decisions. Here are some essential points to consider:

  • Read the rental agreement carefully before signing and clarify any doubts with the landlord.
  • Ensure that the terms and conditions of the agreement are fair and reasonable.
  • Be aware of the notice period and the process for terminating the agreement.

Safety Tips

Staying safe while searching for PG accommodations is vital. Here are some safety tips for your reference:

  • Always meet potential roommates or landlords in public places first.
  • Avoid sharing personal information on social media or with unknown individuals.
  • Carry a fully charged phone and let someone know about your location when visiting new places.
